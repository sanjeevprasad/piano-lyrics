Tips for piano beginners or first time players –

For each line, press first three keys twice and last key once. So,

Line 1:
Press each key of CGA twice and
then G once

Line 2:
Press each key of FED twice and
then C once

Line 3 and 4 same:
Press each key of GFE twice and
then D once

Line 4:
Same as Line 3
